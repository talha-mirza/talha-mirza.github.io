Apollo 8: Moments to Earthrise

The video above the first iteration of the composition running through my Java program to generate the visuals.

With the help of my mentor, Jesse Guessford, and the resources and funding from George Mason University's OSCAR Research Grant, I have been privileged enough to present my work at the National Student Electronic Music Event in February of 2020. I hope to continue building on my work as I grow as a composer.

Using the processing framework to compile Java code, I built a program that analyzes audio in real time, and creates stars that shimmer relative to the music.

The image below is the modular synth that is generating the music you are hearing. Using VCV Rack (a beautiful piece of open source software) I created a patch that randomly determines what pitches to play and parameters to modulate within the limitations that have been set.
