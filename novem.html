NOVEM

A collaborative group made up of artists Lynds Mika and Chaand Soorij, NOVEM is a unique sound in the pop-punk world.

With their debut project "Inside My Hell", NOVEM introduces you into the sound of Lynds Mika's indie punk with Chaand Soorij's ambient and electronic music.
